[DNS-OARC](https://www.dns-oarc.net/) was a two-day conference being held in Charlotte, NC this year.  It is held traditionally aside the major North Amerian Network Operators' Group (NANOG) conference, but while NANOG covers overall networking the DNS-OARC is all about DNS.  It is rare to find a conference that focus on such a specific topic, therefore it is attended by signficant names and parties from around the world.  Being in driving distance, I couldn't pass up the opportunity to attend.
