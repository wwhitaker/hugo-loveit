## My Background

Over the years, I have held many roles and titles, but the core focus has remained the same: adapting to an industry that is constantly evolving. With more than 25 years of experience in information technology, I have built a career centered on network infrastructure, services, security, and software development to glue it together.

### Professional Experience

**University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill** (9+ years)
- *Network Services Architect* (Apr 2023 - Present) - Currently leading network service architecture and strategy for the university's infrastructure.
- *Network DDI Architect* (Mar 2017 - Mar 2023) - Designed and managed DNS, DHCP, and IP address management (DDI) solutions for the university network.

**IBM** (17+ years)
- *Bluemix Network Site Reliability Engineer* (Sep 2016 - Mar 2017) - Supported network architecture for IBM Bluemix Cloud offerings, managing virtual network devices including Vyatta and Fortigate firewalls for customer cloud environments.
- *Network Support Specialist* (Sep 2014 - Sep 2016) - Managed network environments with Cisco ASA firewalls, IBM Proventia IPS, and Juniper EX high-performance switches.
- *Network Security Specialist* (Jul 2007 - Sep 2014) - Managed security compliance of network and server infrastructure, including policy interpretation and education.
- *Network Engineer* (Jan 2000 - Jul 2007) - Served as Technical Lead for 5 years and Administrative Lead for 1 year for the IP Services team, making design decisions for DNS and DHCP services.
- *Network Test Engineer* (Feb 1996 - May 1999) - Participated in cooperative education rotations in a network adapter test lab, developing automation tools and test cases for driver software.
